Cool Math Games Redesign

Goal: Create a responsive interface that reflects the content of the website.

What is Cool Math Games?

Cool Math Games is an online gaming site that promotes clean, safe gaming.

Who is the the target audience?

The target audience is generally younger students from elementary to middle school. This can be assumed because of the clean, family-friendly games that the website provides.

Card Sort

To organize the categories of the types of games, a card sort was done with people in the target audience. They were asked to categorize games and features they would be most interested in playing or value. The search bar was unanimous for importance.

New Design

To give the interface more personality, a retro arcade concept was used. The synthwave style is familiar to the target audience because it is used in games such as Fortnight which is the third most popular games among middle schoolers.

Final Design

The final design changes include:

  • Changing wording of categories
  • shortented navigation bar to four distinct categories
  • On the game page included a save button
