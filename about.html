about me

I am a sophomore at Drexel University as part of the five-year program for User Experience and Interaction Design (B.S.). I became interested in user experience design through an informational session during the college process when I first heard of the term “UX”. I was intrigued by how one could be so versatile in a position like UX, using the creative process to develop a proper and enjoyable experience; that intrigue has led me to Drexel University’s UXID (user experience & interaction design) program.

Although I am most interested in UX, I am also pursuing some of my other passions including filmmaking, which I am currently minoring in. Since I was a child, I was always fascinated with visual storytelling and the way it made me feel. I hope to become a better storyteller that can evoke powerful emotions within my work in and outside of filmmaking.

You can find some of my work on Youtube and Instagram.
